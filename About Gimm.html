About BSU GIMM

What is GIMM?

GIMM, short for Games, Interactive Media & Mobile technology, is an innovative academic program meticulously designed to cultivate the next generation of tech-savvy visionaries. Spearheaded by the esteemed Program Director, Anthony Ellertson, GIMM stands as a beacon for those with an insatiable thirst for knowledge in crafting cutting-edge experiences that seamlessly integrate with our modern digital landscape. At its core, GIMM empowers students to navigate and master the intricate intricacies of various digital platforms, ranging from mobile apps to virtual learning environments and beyond. Through a meticulously curated curriculum spanning four immersive years, GIMM scholars delve into a diverse array of disciplines, including art, computer science, and educational technology, all under the tutelage of industry experts.

The journey through GIMM is not merely an academic pursuit; it is a transformative experience that propels individuals towards excellence in high-growth professions. Armed with a GIMM degree, graduates emerge as sought-after professionals poised to make a profound impact in fields such as mobile app development, video game design, and web development. With an average salary ranging from $80,000 to $90,000 per year, GIMM alumni are primed to thrive in the dynamic landscape of technology. More than just a program, GIMM represents a community of forward-thinkers and innovators, where creativity flourishes, and boundaries are continuously pushed.
